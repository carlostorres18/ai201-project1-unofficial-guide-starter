# Open Syllabus — Homepage

Source URL: https://opensyllabus.org/

Mapping the college curriculum across 32.9 million syllabi. Open Syllabus is a massive non-profit archive of the main activity of higher education: teaching. It provides top-down views of the curriculum across thousands of schools to support curricular innovation, lifelong learning, and student success. Open Syllabus works to expand the boundaries of open education. Most of its tools are free to use.
